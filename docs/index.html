Shuttle Buses to the Wedding Venue

The shuttles to Sunspot Lodge will start loading at 4:30pm on Saturday. The shuttle location is at the Fraser Crossing/Founders Pointe bus stop which is marked with a RED X on the map. Parking for wedding guests who are not staying in a Winter Park lodging is available at the parking garage labeled "FREE" to the Northwest of the FCFP shuttle bus stop.
